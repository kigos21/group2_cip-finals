Gaudete Et Exsultate

Gaudete et Exsultate is a document written by Pope Francis, which was published in April 2018. The title of the document means "Rejoice and Be Glad" in Latin, and it is a call to all Christians to strive for holiness in their daily lives. A papal document that seeks to inspire and encourage Christians to live lives of holiness, by embracing their own unique path to sanctity, avoiding false ideologies, living out the Beatitudes, engaging in prayer and discernment, and working for justice and peace in the world.

Ways of Living Gaudete Et Exsultate

The Beatitudes

The Beatitudes are a series of teachings that Jesus Christ gave during his Sermon on the Mount, as recorded in the Gospel of Matthew (5:3-12). They are considered to be some of the most important teachings of Jesus, and they describe the attitudes and behaviors that lead to true happiness and blessedness. The Beatitudes are an important theme in the papal document Gaudete Et Exsultate because they are seen as a blueprint for living a life of holiness. In the document, Pope Francis emphasizes that the Beatitudes are not just a set of moral guidelines or a series of impossible demands, but rather a call to a way of life that is centered on humility, compassion, and mercy.

Seek opportunities for service

Holiness is not just about what we believe or how we feel, but how we act in the world. Seeking out opportunities for service, volunteering, and giving back to our communities can be a way to grow in holiness and put our faith into action.

Gaudete Et Exsultate emphasizes the importance of building strong relationships with others and working towards common goals. It also stresses the importance of caring for those who are most vulnerable and marginalized in society.

Become humble and pray.

Prayer is not just about reciting prose but it is also about having a conversation with God. Every second of your life is given to you by God, so never take it for granted. Thank God for giving you a chance in life. For every mistake you've done, every sins you commit, every evil thoughts you imagine, you ask God for forgiveness. Then lastly, you can ask God more of his blessing for you, your family, and love ones. Learn to appreciate all the conversations you have with God because God will always hear you.

Understand the true meaning of holiness

Many people misunderstood the true definition of holiness. One must understood that you must take the biblical definition. Holiness mean being set apart for God. Every intention, action, thought, and motives are in the purpose that benefits and favor God. You must make it a habit to put all your thoughts in pursuit of God's favor to be truly holy.

Find joy in the small things

Remember that joy can be found in the simplest things in life. Take time to appreciate the beauty of nature, the kindness of a stranger, or the laughter of a child. By cultivating a sense of wonder and gratitude for the small things in life, you can experience a deep and lasting joy that is at the heart of holiness.
