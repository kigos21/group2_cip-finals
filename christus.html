Christus Vivit

 

If you are young in years, but feel weak, weary or disillusioned, ask Jesus to renew you.

Christus Vivit

Christus Vivit, highlights Pope Francis's desire to have a Christocentric focus with his audience. It reflects on the theme of young, faith, and vocational discernment, exploring on various aspects of young people's lives and offers guidance on how they can deepen their relationship with Christ and discern their vocations. Throughout the exhortation, he constantly invites the young to an encounter and friendship with Jesus. It is a call to hope, to action, and to a deeper encounter with the living Christ.

Ways of Living Christus Vivit

Develop a personal relationship with Christ

One of the key messages of "Christus Vivit" is that Christ is alive and present in our lives. To experience this reality, it's important to cultivate a personal relationship with Christ through prayer, reading the Bible, and participating in the sacraments. It requires a commited ongoing spiritual growth and openness to the presence of Christ in daily life. This way, we can live out His message of love and compassion in the world.

Serve others

Christus Vivit emphasizes the importance of sercing others as a way of following Christ's example. Christ taught us to love our neighbors as ourselves and to serve others. By volunteering, helping those in need, and spreading kindness, we can make Christ's love tangible in the world and build communities, promote social justice, develop humility, and foster personal growth.

Live a virtuous life

As Christians, we are called to live virtuous lives and to strive for holiness. By cultivating virtues such as love, compassion, patience, and humility, we can become more like Christ and bear witness to his presence in our lives. Living a virtuous life is essential to Christus Vivit as it helps us to grow in holiness, become more Christ-like, and make a positive difference in the world.

Share the Gospel

Sharing the Gospel is a paramount importance in Christus Vivit as it is a way to spread the message of God's love and salvation to all people. Christ commissioned his disciples to go out and spread the Good News to all nations. By sharing our faith with others, we can help others encounter Christ and experience the joy of the Gospel. It also helps to us to build stronger communities of faith, bringring people together in a shared mission and purpose.

READ MORE

Embrace diversity

In "Christus Vivit," Pope Francis emphasizes the importance of embracing diversity and welcoming others into our communities. Embracing Diversity reflects God's diverse creation and acknowledges that every person has something unique and valuable to contribute. By reaching out to those who are marginalized or different from us, we can break barriers that divide people, such as racism, prejudice, and discrimination, and we can build bridge, encourage mutual respect and understanding, and create a more inclusive, Christ-centered society.
